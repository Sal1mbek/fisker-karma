Our Story

Fisker Karma is a pioneering electric vehicle (EV) manufacturer that has been at the forefront of sustainable transportation since its inception. Founded in 2007 by Henrik Fisker, our company has always been driven by the vision of creating beautiful, high-performance vehicles that have a positive impact on the environment.

Our journey began with the development of the Fisker Karma, a luxury plug-in hybrid electric vehicle that combined cutting-edge technology with elegant design. It quickly gained recognition for its innovative powertrain and unique aesthetics.

Over the years, we have continued to push the boundaries of EV technology and design. We are committed to reducing our carbon footprint and promoting sustainable mobility solutions for the future.
